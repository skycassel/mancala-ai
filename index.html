How to Play Mancala

  1. The goal is to collect more stones than your opponent.
  2. On your turn, select one of your pits to pick up all stones from it.
  3. Moving counter-clockwise, distribute the stones one by one into each pit, including your mancala (the large pit on your right). Skip your opponent's mancala when distributing stones.
  4. If your last stone lands in your own mancala, you get another turn.
  5. If your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, you capture that stone and all stones in the opposite pit.
  6. The game ends when all pits on one side are empty.
  7. Any remaining stones go to the player who owns that side.
  8. The player with the most stones in their mancala wins!
